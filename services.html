Services

Free Consultation

This is a free 60 min slot to allow you to see if personal training is for you. During this we will sit and chat for around 40 mins. Discussing your goals, how I can help you in achieving them and which PT package may suit you best. For the final 20 mins, we will then get out on the gym floor if you wish and go through anything you want. This will again give you a feel for what 1-2-1 sessions are all about!

One Off 1-2-1 Sessions

This is a one off 60 minute 1-2-1 PT session. Prior to this, we can discuss what it is your after. Weather you want to learn a certain movement, how to use set machines, how to use Free weights etc.. Or your just looking for an increase in intensity! Then I can plan the session and we can make sure you get as much out of it as you can.

Monthly PT Packages

These are packages for anyone who’s serious about achieving their goals. Packages range from 1 session per week up to 4 sessions per week. Also included in each package will be a tailored training plan for outside our 1-2-1 sessions. A nutrition plan fully tailored to you and your goal! 24/7 contact via WhatsApp with myself for any advice/support you may need.

Online Coaching

Monthly online coaching is a great way to get yourself on track give your self some accountability without the 1-2-1 training sessions. In this package, you will receive a tailored training & nutrition plan. 24/7 contact via WhatsApp with myself for any advice/support you may need. Weekly check-ins we’re we will assess your progress and alter the plans if needed for the following week to assure you keep progressing towards that desired goal.

Custom Training Plan

This is a one off training plan that will be tailored to your body and your goal. It will also be based on the equipment you have available at your training facility. It will include detailed sets, Reps, tempos and training methods.

Tailored Nutrition Plan

This is a one off nutrition plan that will be tailored to you and your goal. Foods you enjoy can be included. Foods you don’t like we can avoid. This will be specifically tailored to you and your food preferences whilst making sure it will aid you in achieving that end goal!
